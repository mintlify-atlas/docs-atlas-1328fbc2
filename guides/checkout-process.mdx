The Flutter Billing App provides a streamlined checkout process using real-time barcode scanning and automated calculations. This guide covers the complete workflow from scanning items to printing receipts.

## The Home Screen Scanner

The home screen is divided into two main sections:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Top Section" icon="camera">
    Live camera view for scanning product barcodes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bottom Section" icon="shopping-cart">
    Cart display showing scanned items and total price
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Scanning Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the Camera">
    When you open the app, the camera starts automatically. If it's turned off, you'll see a message "Camera is turned off" with a button to turn it on.
    
    **Camera Controls:**
    - **Flash icon**: Toggle flashlight on/off for low-light scanning
    - **Camera icon**: Turn camera on/off to save battery
    - **Settings icon**: Access app settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position the Barcode">
    Point your camera at the product barcode. A scanning frame with green corners appears in the center of the screen.
    
    <Info>
      The app automatically detects and reads barcodes when they're within the scanning frame. No need to tap or capture manually.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Product Added to Cart">
    When a barcode is successfully scanned:
    
    - Your phone **vibrates** briefly (if vibration is enabled)
    - The product automatically appears in the cart below
    - The total price updates immediately
    
    <Note>
      **Cooldown Protection**: The same barcode can only be scanned once every 2 seconds to prevent accidental duplicate scans.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Handle Scanning Errors">
    If a product isn't found in your inventory, you'll see an error message:
    
    ```
    Product not found: [barcode]
    ```
    
    This means the barcode isn't in your inventory yet. You'll need to add it first from Settings > Products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing the Cart

As you scan items, they appear in the cart at the bottom of the screen.

### Cart Display

Each cart item shows:
- **Product Name**: Clear product identifier
- **Unit Price**: Price per item (₹X.XX format)
- **Quantity Controls**: Buttons to adjust quantity
- **Total Price**: Updated in real-time at the top

### Adjusting Quantities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Increase Quantity">
    Tap the **+ button** on the right side of any cart item to add one more unit.
    
    <Tip>
      You can also scan the same barcode multiple times to increase quantity (with 2-second cooldown between scans).
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Decrease Quantity">
    Tap the **- button** to reduce quantity by one.
    
    - If quantity is greater than 1, it decreases by one
    - If quantity is 1, the item is removed from the cart entirely
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cart Summary

At the top of the cart panel, you'll see:

```
Scanned Items
X items total

TOTAL PRICE
₹XXX.XX
```

This updates automatically as you add, remove, or adjust items.

## Proceeding to Checkout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Cart">
    Before proceeding, verify that:
    - All items are correct
    - Quantities are accurate
    - Total price looks right
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Review Order">
    When you're ready, tap the **Review Order** button at the bottom of the screen.
    
    <Warning>
      This button is only enabled when you have at least one item in your cart.
    </Warning>
    
    The camera automatically stops when you navigate to checkout to save battery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Checkout Page

The checkout page displays a detailed order summary and payment options.

### Order Summary Table

You'll see a formatted table with:

| Product Name | Price | Total |
|--------------|-------|-------|
| 2 x Basmati Rice | ₹299.00 | ₹598.00 |
| 1 x Cooking Oil | ₹450.00 | ₹450.00 |

### Payment QR Code

If you've configured UPI details in your shop settings:

<Steps>
  <Step title="QR Code Generated">
    A UPI payment QR code is automatically generated with:
    - Your UPI ID
    - Shop name
    - Exact order total
    - Currency (INR)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customer Scans to Pay">
    The customer can scan this QR code with any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.) to complete payment.
    
    <Info>
      The QR code uses the standard UPI deep link format: `upi://pay?pa=[UPI_ID]&pn=[SHOP_NAME]&am=[AMOUNT]&cu=INR`
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Grand Total Display

At the bottom, the grand total is prominently displayed:

```
GRAND TOTAL
₹1,048.00
```

## Printing the Receipt

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Printer">
    Ensure your Bluetooth thermal printer is connected in Settings. See the [Printer Setup Guide](/guides/printer-setup) for details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Print Receipt">
    Tap the **Print Receipt** button at the bottom of the checkout page.
    
    The button shows a loading indicator while printing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receipt Prints">
    The receipt prints with the following information:
    
    - **Shop Name** (centered, bold, large text)
    - **Address Lines** (if configured)
    - **Phone Number**
    - **Date and Time** (format: DD-MM-YYYY HH:MM AM/PM)
    - **Itemized List** with quantities and totals
    - **Grand Total** (right-aligned, bold)
    - **Footer Text** (custom message, centered)
    
    <Tip>
      If you see "Printer not connected," the app will attempt to auto-connect using your saved printer settings.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Success Confirmation">
    When printing succeeds, you'll see a green success message:
    
    ```
    Printed successfully
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Completing the Transaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Return to Home">
    Tap the **back arrow** in the top-left corner or use your device's back button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cart Clears Automatically">
    When you leave the checkout page, the cart is automatically cleared and you're ready for the next customer.
    
    <Warning>
      Going back from checkout will clear your cart. Make sure you've printed the receipt before leaving this page.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ready for Next Customer">
    The camera resumes automatically and you can start scanning items for the next order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Barcode won't scan">
    - Ensure the barcode is clean and not damaged
    - Try enabling the flashlight for better visibility
    - Make sure the barcode is within the green scanning frame
    - Check that the product exists in your inventory
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Print failed error">
    - Verify printer is turned on and has paper
    - Check Bluetooth connection in Settings
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting the printer
    - Ensure the printer is within Bluetooth range
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="QR code not showing">
    You need to configure your UPI ID in Shop Details. Go to Settings > Shop Details and add your UPI ID.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong item total">
    - Verify product prices in Product Management
    - Check if quantity is correct
    - Update product pricing if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

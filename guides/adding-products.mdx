The Flutter Billing App allows you to easily add products to your inventory using barcode scanning or manual entry. This guide walks you through the complete process.

## Opening the Product Management Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    From the home screen, tap the **Settings** icon in the top-right corner of the scanner view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Products">
    In the Settings page, tap on **Products** under the Management section. This will show you all existing products and options to manage them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a New Product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the Add Button">
    On the Product Management page, tap the floating **+** button in the bottom-right corner. This opens the Add Product form.
    
    <Note>
      The floating action button is styled in your app's primary color with a white plus icon.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter or Scan Barcode">
    You have two options for entering the barcode:
    
    **Option 1: Scan with Camera**
    - Tap the **QR code scanner** icon next to the barcode field
    - Point your camera at the product barcode
    - The barcode value will automatically populate the field
    
    **Option 2: Manual Entry**
    - Type the barcode directly into the barcode field
    - This is useful if the barcode is damaged or hard to scan
    
    <Warning>
      Each barcode must be unique. If you try to add a product with an existing barcode, you'll see an error: "Product with barcode \"XXX\" already exists!"
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Product Name">
    Type the product name in the **Product Name** field. 
    
    **Tips:**
    - Use clear, descriptive names (e.g., "Basmati Rice" instead of just "Rice")
    - The field automatically capitalizes the first letter of each word
    - Product names are required and cannot be empty
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set the Price">
    Enter the product price in the **Price** field.
    
    **Format:**
    - Enter price with decimals (e.g., 299.99)
    - The currency symbol ₹ is automatically added
    - Price must be a valid positive number
    
    <Info>
      The price you set here will be used during checkout calculations.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the Product">
    Tap the **Add Product** button at the bottom of the screen.
    
    The product is immediately saved and you'll be returned to the Product Management page where you can see your new product in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Your Products

After adding products, they appear in the Product Management page with the following information:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Product Name" icon="tag">
    Displayed prominently at the top of each product card
  </Card>
  <Card title="Price" icon="indian-rupee-sign">
    Shown in ₹ format with two decimal places
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit Option" icon="pen-to-square">
    Blue edit icon to modify product details
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete Option" icon="trash">
    Red delete icon to remove products
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Searching for Products

The Product Management page includes a search feature:

- Use the search bar at the top to filter products by name or barcode
- Tap the **QR scanner icon** to scan a barcode and automatically search for that product
- The list updates in real-time as you type

<Tip>
  If no products match your search, you'll see the message "No products match your search."
</Tip>

## What Happens Next

Once products are added to your inventory:

1. They become available for scanning during checkout
2. When you scan their barcode on the home screen, they're automatically added to the cart
3. The product name and price are displayed on receipts
4. You can edit or delete them anytime from the Product Management page

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Barcode scanner not working">
    Make sure you've granted camera permissions to the app. The scanner requires camera access to read barcodes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Duplicate barcode error">
    Each product must have a unique barcode. If you see this error, check if the product already exists or use a different barcode.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Price validation error">
    Ensure you're entering a valid number format. Use decimals for cents (e.g., 99.50 not 99.5).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Effective inventory management is crucial for smooth checkout operations. This guide covers all inventory management features including editing products, updating prices, searching, and deleting items.

## Accessing Product Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the home screen, tap the **Settings** icon in the top-right corner of the scanner view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Products">
    Under the **Management** section, tap on **Products**.
    
    The subtitle reads: "Manage stock and barcodes"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Product List">
    You'll see all your products displayed as cards with:
    - Product name (bold, prominent)
    - Price in ₹ format
    - Edit and Delete action buttons
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Searching and Filtering Products

The Product Management page includes powerful search capabilities.

### Text Search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Search Bar">
    At the top of the Product Management page, you'll find a search bar with the placeholder "Scan or enter barcode."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type to Filter">
    Start typing a product name or barcode.
    
    The product list filters in real-time as you type, showing only matching items.
    
    **Search matches:**
    - Product names (case-insensitive)
    - Barcodes (exact or partial match)
    
    <Tip>
      The search automatically capitalizes the first letter of each word for product names.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    As you type, the list updates to show only products that match your query.
    
    If no products match, you'll see: "No products match your search."
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Barcode Search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Scanner Icon">
    Next to the search bar, tap the **QR code scanner** icon (blue background).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan a Barcode">
    Point your camera at any product barcode.
    
    When scanned:
    - If the product exists, the search bar auto-fills with the product name
    - If the product doesn't exist, the barcode value is placed in the search bar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Matching Product">
    The product list filters to show the scanned product, making it easy to quickly locate and edit items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Product Details

You can update product names and prices at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Product">
    Use the search feature or scroll through the product list to find the item you want to edit.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Edit Icon">
    On the product card, tap the **blue edit icon** (pencil) on the right side.
    
    This opens the Edit Product page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Barcode (Immutable)">
    At the top of the Edit page, you'll see a blue-highlighted box displaying the product's barcode.
    
    <Warning>
      **Barcodes cannot be changed** once a product is created. If you need a different barcode, you must delete this product and create a new one.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Product Name">
    The **Product Name** field shows the current name.
    
    - Tap the field to edit
    - Change the name as needed
    - The field auto-capitalizes words
    - Names cannot be empty
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Price">
    The **Price** field shows the current price.
    
    - Tap to edit the price
    - Enter the new price with decimals (e.g., 349.99)
    - The ₹ symbol is automatically added
    - Price must be a valid positive number
    
    <Info>
      Price changes take effect immediately after saving. Future scans will use the new price.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Tap the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the screen.
    
    You'll be returned to the Product Management page with a success message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting Products

Remove products that are no longer sold or were added by mistake.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Product">
    Find the product you want to delete using search or by scrolling through the list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Delete Icon">
    On the product card, tap the **red delete icon** (trash can) on the right side.
    
    A confirmation dialog appears.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    The dialog asks: "Are you sure you want to delete [Product Name]?"
    
    **Options:**
    - **Cancel**: Keep the product and close the dialog
    - **Delete**: Permanently remove the product (red text)
    
    <Warning>
      Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Deleted products must be re-added manually if needed.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Product Removed">
    After confirming, the product is immediately removed from your inventory.
    
    A success message confirms the deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Product Display

Each product in the list is displayed as a card with the following elements:

### Card Layout

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Product Information">
    **Left Side:**
    - Product name (16px, bold weight)
    - Price in ₹ with 2 decimal places (gray text)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Action Buttons">
    **Right Side:**
    - **Edit button**: Blue icon with rounded background
    - **Delete button**: Red icon with rounded background
    
    Both buttons are 8px apart for easy tapping
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Visual Design

- **White background** with rounded corners (12px radius)
- **Light gray border** for subtle separation
- **Box shadow** for depth (4px blur, 2px offset)
- **12px spacing** between cards for clean layout

## Tips for Efficient Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use Consistent Naming">
    Develop a naming convention for products:
    - Include brand names when relevant
    - Use standard abbreviations
    - Be consistent with capitalization
    
    **Example:** "Basmati Rice 5kg" instead of "rice" or "RICE"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Prices Before Saving">
    Double-check price updates to avoid customer disputes:
    - Ensure decimal placement is correct
    - Verify against current market prices
    - Update related product prices together
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Clean Up Regularly">
    Schedule weekly or monthly inventory reviews:
    - Remove discontinued products
    - Update seasonal pricing
    - Verify all barcodes scan correctly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test After Changes">
    After making significant updates:
    - Scan a few products on the home screen
    - Verify prices display correctly
    - Test checkout to confirm calculations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Inventory Tasks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bulk price increase for multiple products">
    Currently, the app doesn't support bulk editing. To update multiple products:
    
    1. Use search to find related products
    2. Edit each product individually
    3. Update the price and save
    4. Repeat for all products in the category
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Changing a product's barcode">
    Barcodes cannot be modified after creation. To change a barcode:
    
    1. Note the product's current name and price
    2. Delete the product from inventory
    3. Add a new product with the correct barcode
    4. Set the same name and price
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure no one is scanning the old barcode during this process.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Recovering deleted products">
    Deleted products cannot be recovered. You must manually re-add them:
    
    1. Go to Product Management
    2. Tap the + button
    3. Re-enter all product details
    4. Scan or type the barcode
    5. Save the product
    
    <Tip>
      Consider keeping a backup list of important products and their details outside the app.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Handling products with the same name">
    You can have multiple products with the same name if they have different barcodes.
    
    **Example:** "Basmati Rice" can exist with different barcodes for 1kg, 5kg, and 10kg packages.
    
    Each barcode is unique and links to its specific product entry with its own price.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Error Handling

### Validation Errors

When editing products, you might encounter these validation errors:

<Warning>
  **"Please enter a name"** - Product name field is empty
  
  **"Please enter a valid price"** - Price field is empty or contains invalid characters
  
  **"Product with barcode \"XXX\" already exists"** - Attempted to add duplicate barcode (on new product only)
</Warning>

### Success Messages

When operations complete successfully:

<Check>**Product updated** - Changes saved successfully</Check>
<Check>**Product deleted** - Item removed from inventory</Check>
<Check>**Product added** - New item created successfully</Check>

## Data Persistence

All inventory data is stored locally on your device using Hive database:

- **Automatic saving**: Changes are saved immediately
- **Offline-first**: No internet connection required
- **Device-specific**: Data doesn't sync across devices
- **Persistent**: Data survives app restarts and updates

<Info>
  Currently, there's no cloud sync or backup feature. Make sure to keep your device safe and consider periodically documenting your product list.
</Info>
